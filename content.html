HITMAN 3 - Deluxe Edition


HITMAN 3's first content update is here! We're adding new and free content to the game every week in Feburary for all HITMAN 3 players - plus owners of the Deluxe Edition will get access to two new Deluxe Escalations that will unlock unique suits and items.
